This component you're reading is a text component. All components take this form of a title and body - except a blank component. However it's important to realise that title and body attributes are not needed.

Take for instance the media component opposite or below on a smaller device - has no body attribute. Instead of the HTML being rendered Adapt simply doesn't render it. The title attribute is simply a break tag to push the media component level with the opposite component.

This new method of injection makes it easy to read :
  • HTML Markup
  • White spaces
  • Long long bodys of text in general!!
